NZ Police Vehicle Theft Analysis

View Dashboard

The Problem

Identifying patterns in criminal activity is essential for resource allocation. This project explores a 6-month dataset from the New Zealand Police Department to uncover high-risk theft profiles and hotspots.

The Data

Source: NZ Police Dept (via Kaggle).
Attributes: Vehicle type, make, year, color, date stolen, and geographic region.

Methodology

  • SQL Joins & CTEs: Linking theft incidents with granular vehicle attributes.
  • Advanced Aggregations: Calculating frequency across regions and timeframes.
  • Looker Studio: Transforming raw SQL outputs into an executive-level dashboard.

Key Insights

95%

of stolen vehicles were Standard models, while Luxury cars accounted for only 4%.

36%

Total thefts occurred in Auckland, identifying it as the primary hotspot.

M/T

Thefts peaked on Mondays and Tuesdays, suggesting higher vulnerability after weekends.

Top Makes: Toyota (15.7%), Nissan (10.6%).

PISA 2012: Global Student Performance

View Analysis

The Problem

As part of the Udacity Data Analyst Nanodegree, this project investigates the PISA 2012 dataset. The goal was to identify which variables—ranging from gender and geography to teacher practices—most significantly impact the academic success of 15-year-old students globally.

The Data

Volume: 510,000 student records with 636 unique features.
Focus: Socio-economic status, school resources, and regional disparities (OECD vs. Non-OECD).
Download Raw Dataset

Methodology

  • Data Wrangling: Cleaning a massive 600+ column dataset using Pandas.
  • Multivariate Visualization: Using Seaborn and Matplotlib to find correlations between wealth and test scores.
  • Hypothesis Testing: Statistical validation of performance gaps between genders and regions.

Global Insights

Parental Influence: High parental education levels proved to be the strongest predictor of student success across all tested regions.

Digital Divide: Access to school computers improved scores, yet students in tech-equipped schools who *never* used them scored the lowest.

Regional Excellence: Students in East Asia and Western Europe significantly outperformed the global average in Mathematics.

KCSE 2023: Sub-County Performance Analysis

View Full Report

The Problem

Commissioned by the Sub-County Director of Education, this project required a comprehensive performance audit of 19 local schools. The objective was to compare 2023 KCSE results against historical data (2021-2022) to identify which institutions were improving and where urgent intervention was needed.

The Data

Source: Official Sub-County Education Records.
Volume: Records for 2,400 students across 19 institutions.
Key Metrics: Mean grade trends, Subject-specific performance, and Gender-based achievement gaps.

Methodology

  • Power Pivot & DAX: Built a relational data model to handle multi-year comparisons efficiently.
  • Improvement Indexing: Developed custom formulas to calculate year-over-year growth per school.
  • Stakeholder Reporting: Created a clean, interactive dashboard for non-technical education officials to track school rankings.

Performance Metrics

+13%

Increase in students attaining University Entry Grades (C+) compared to the previous cycle.

17%

Performance lead by Female Students, who consistently outperformed males in high-tier grade categories.

Critical

Identified a 50% surge in Grade E results, signaling a widening achievement gap that requires policy intervention.

Top Schools: Ribe Boys & Kombeni Girls.

POWER BI: SPROCKET CENTRAL ANALYTICS

Sprocket Central Marketing

The Problem

The project involves analyzing Sprocket Central Pty Ltd's datasets (demographics, addresses, transactions) to optimize marketing strategies. The goal is to identify trends and high-value customers among 1,000 new prospects to drive business growth.

The Data

Source: Sprocket Central Pty Ltd.
Tables: Customer Demographics, Addresses, 3 Months Transactions.

Methodology

  • Feature Engineering to create customer segments.
  • Predictive modeling for purchase likelihood.
  • DAX measures for Gross Profit and RFM analysis.

Insights

Customer demographics and purchasing behavior.

  • Target Pool: 382 customers projected to drive 29K bike purchases.
  • Demographics: Key targets are Females (40-50) and Males (20-30 & 60-70).
  • Location: NSW (56%), VIC (26%), QLD (21%) are top regions.
  • Brands: Solex (20%), Giant Bicycles (17%), WeareA2B (16.8%) lead preferences.

TABLEAU: EUROPEAN SOCCER LEAGUE

Interactive Tableau Dashboard (JS Loaded)

Soccer Database (2008-2016)

The Problem

Exploring a soccer database from Kaggle to reveal key insights into Europe's major leagues (2008-09 to 2015-16). The project presents interactive league tables in Tableau, showcasing team performances and year-to-year improvements.

The Data

Source: Kaggle Ultimate Soccer Database.
Structure: 7 tables, 25,000+ matches, 10,000+ players.
Download Database

Premier League Trends

Performance trends from 2008-2016.

  • Winners: Chelsea, Man Utd, Man City, and Leicester City. Leicester's 2016 win was historic (81 pts).
  • Top 4: Arsenal consistently top 4 but no title. Spurs and Liverpool fluctuated.
  • YoY Improvement: Napoli (+0.95) and PSG (+0.84) showed most growth. Aston Villa and FC Zürich had biggest declines.

SQL: AIRLINES SALES & OPERATIONS

Airlines Insights

The Problem

Analysis of a Kaggle SQLite dataset to uncover revenue drivers, seasonal trends, and delay patterns. Built interactive dashboards to communicate operational insights clearly.

The Data

Source: Kaggle Airlines Dataset.
Scope: Ticket sales, flight schedules, delay codes, airport info.

Methodology

  • Complex SQL queries using CTEs and Window Functions.
  • Python (Pandas) for initial EDA.
  • Looker Studio for final visualization.

Insights

Revenue and operational efficiency findings.

  • Airports: Domodedovo (1,986M), Sheremetyevo (1,579M), Tolmachevo (799M) are top revenue hubs.
  • Seasonality: July (63%) and August (34%) had highest sales.
  • Delays: Ulyanovsk East Airport had most delays. Flights to Moscow City and Kazan City experienced >3hr delays.
